Hello! My name is Aishatu Salisu Suleiman. I'm a Computer Science graduate and an aspiring software engineer from Bauchi State, Nigeria. Technology has always fascinated me from how websites work to how mobile apps are built. That curiosity led me to pursue a B.Tech in Computer Science, and now I am continuing my learning journey at AltSchool Africa under the School of Engineering. My decision to join AltSchool came from a strong desire to grow beyond the classroom. I did not just want to understand the theory behind technology. I wanted to learn how to build real things that solve real problems. AltSchool's hands-on approach, communitydriven learning, and project based system aligned perfectly with what I was looking for in a tech learning experience. I have a genuine passion for coding and learning. I am the kind of person who loves figuring out how things work, breaking problems down, and experimenting until I find solutions. I believe that the best way to grow in tech is to stay curious and to never stop learning. That is why I always seek opportunities to expand my knowledge and challenge myself with new tools, languages, and concepts. One of my goals is to become not just a developer, but a well-rounded problem-solver someone who can work on projects that impact people's lives in meaningful ways. I am also passionate about knowledge sharing. I enjoy teaching and helping others, especially women and young girls who are interested in joining the tech space. I believe representation matters, and I want to be a role model for those who feel like tech is not for them. When I am not writing code or reading tech articles, I love listening to gists and conversations that offer different perspectives on life. I find joy in simple things connecting with people, understanding their stories, and learning from them. The journey ahead is long, but I am excited about every step. I know that with dedication, support, and the right resources, I will be able to achieve my dreams and contribute meaningfully to the world of technology.
